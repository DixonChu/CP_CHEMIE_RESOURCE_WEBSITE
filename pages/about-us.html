Leaders always look toward the future.

CP Chemie was founded in 2010 in Puchong, Selangor, Malaysia. Our journey began with a vision to develop thermoplastic elastomer (TPE) industry.

In our formative years, we focused on building a robust foundation by investing in state-of-the-art technology and skilled talent. Our products were designed to meet the growing demand for high-performance TPE solutions across various industries, including automotive, consumer goods, and industrial applications as well as medical industry. We also expand business from local supply to export markets in Asia Pacific and Middle East.
